FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do I need any technical knowledge to use SmartOps Foundry? ▼

No. If you can use email, you can use SmartOps. The dashboard walks you through everything step by step, and the AI handles all message drafting — you just review and approve.

Can I see what messages look like before they're sent? ▼

Absolutely. Every message is drafted by AI and shown to you for approval. You can edit the text, change the tone, or reject it entirely. Nothing goes out without your green light.

How many businesses can I manage? ▼

Depends on your plan. Starter = 1 business, Professional = up to 5, Agency = unlimited. You can upgrade or downgrade anytime — no long-term contracts.

Does it work with my existing phone number and email? ▼

Yes. Connect your existing Twilio number for SMS, or use your current email provider via SMTP. SmartOps works alongside your existing setup — it doesn't replace it.

What if I don't have Twilio or an email provider yet? ▼

No problem. SmartOps runs in "simulation mode" — you can test every automation, see AI-drafted messages, and preview the full experience without connecting any external provider. When you're ready, just add your credentials.

Is my business data secure? ▼

Yes. Passwords are hashed with scrypt (industry-standard), sessions are encrypted cookie-based, and each client's data is stored in its own isolated directory. We never share or mix data between accounts.
